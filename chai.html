Slow Cooker Chai


Throw in a combination of chai spices, add a sweet cinnamon oil that calls to mind brown sugar, and the result is a wonderfully balanced sweet and spicy treat you can enjoy rain or shine. Chai tea is a fragrant, spicy tea that may help boost heart health, reduce blood sugar levels, aid digestion and help with weight loss. Although most of these health benefits are backed by science, it's worth noting that they are generally linked to the ingredients used in chai tea rather than chai tea itself.
